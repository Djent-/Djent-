How to enable Dell Powershare USB Charging on Early 2015 Dell XPS 13

  • 1. Power off your computer
  • 2. Power on your computer
  • 3. Mash/hold F12 while the computer boots
  • 4. Enter BIOS Settings
  • 5. Expand USB Settings
  • 6. Click on Powershare Settings and check the Powershare box

While I was in my BIOS settings, I deactivated BlueTooth and the webcam, as I do not use them anyway.

I enabled this in order to use my laptop as an external battery charger while playing Pokemon Go. Seems it will work well.
